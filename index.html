ABOUT

This site was originally meant to function as a place where I could experiment and try out different ideas and concepts. However, after creating the mini-projects on this site, I ended up keeping them on here instead of deleting them.

Also, after filling this site with a bunch of mini-projects it seemed like a waste to just have these on here without anyone seeing them. So I've decided to treat this site like an art gallery of sorts where others can visit and view the different projects.

Some of the ideas I've experimented with and now showcase include:
  • Different Types of Parallax Animations
  • 3D Object Creation and Animation
  • Hover Animations
  • etc...
Anyways, look around till your hearts content and enjoy.
